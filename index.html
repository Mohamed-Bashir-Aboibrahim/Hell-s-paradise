WHAT IS HELL'S PARADISE?

In the Edo period of Japan, the most vicious criminals are offered a chance at pardon if they can retrieve the Elixir of Life from a mysterious island known as Shinsenkyo. Gabimaru the Hollow, a legendary ninja assassin, is one such criminal sent to this island that promises immortality... and death.

Accompanied by his executioner Yamada Asaemon Sagiri, Gabimaru discovers the island is inhabited by monstrous creatures and former explorers who have been horrifically transformed. The search for the elixir becomes a brutal fight for survival against the island's nightmarish inhabitants and the criminals' own dark natures.

Based on the hit manga by Yuji Kaku, Hell's Paradise blends historical action with body horror in a deadly battle royale where only the strongest—or most cunning—will survive.

KEY ARCS

Hell's Paradise unfolds across several intense story arcs as the criminals explore the deadly island. Some of the most memorable include:

The Arrival

The criminals first set foot on Shinsenkyo, immediately encountering the island's deadly flora and fauna that defy nature.

The Tensen

The group encounters the island's god-like rulers, beings of immense power who view humans as mere specimens.

Gabimaru's Past

Flashbacks reveal Gabimaru's brutal upbringing as a ninja and his relationship with his wife that humanizes the "Hollow" man.

Through these harrowing trials, Hell's Paradise explores themes of redemption, survival, and what it means to be human in the face of unimaginable horror.

MAIN CHARACTERS

The expedition to Shinsenkyo brings together deadly criminals and their executioner minders in an uneasy alliance. The main characters include:

Gabimaru

Known as "Gabimaru the Hollow," this legendary ninja assassin claims to want nothing more than to return to his wife, but his brutal skills suggest otherwise.

Sagiri

Yamada Asaemon Sagiri is Gabimaru's executioner and minder, a skilled swordswoman struggling with the morality of her duties.

The Tensen

Mysterious god-like beings who rule Shinsenkyo, capable of manipulating flesh and Tao energy with terrifying precision.

Yuzuriha

A female ninja and master of disguise whose cheerful demeanor hides a deadly killer with her own agenda.

These characters form the core of a brutal survival story where trust is as dangerous as the island itself.
